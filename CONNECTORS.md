# Connectors Guide

The Job Search Co-Pilot works standalone with web search and persistent memory, but connecting external tools will supercharge your job discovery and pipeline management.

## Available Connectors

### ~~LinkedIn~~

**With Connector:** Auto-sync your profile, import job listings from your feed, send connection requests, and track recruiter outreach.

**Without Connector:** You'll manually share job links and update your profile information through conversation. Web search still uncovers relevant LinkedIn roles.

---

### ~~Naukri~~

**With Connector:** Direct access to Indian job board listings, seamless applications, and saved preferences.

**Without Connector:** You provide job links and descriptions manually. The plugin researches roles using web search and maintains your preferences in memory.

---

### ~~Greenhouse / Lever~~

**With Connector:** Automatic tracking of applications across ATS systems, interview stage updates, and offer details.

**Without Connector:** You update pipeline status manually with `/job-search:update`. The plugin tracks everything in PIPELINE.md.

---

### ~~Google Calendar~~

**With Connector:** Automatic interview scheduling, calendar synchronization, and interview reminders.

**Without Connector:** You share interview details in conversation. The plugin tracks dates and sends proactive reminders via briefings.

---

### ~~Gmail / Outlook~~

**With Connector:** Automatic tracking of recruiter emails, outreach history, and follow-up scheduling.

**Without Connector:** You paste relevant emails or summaries. The plugin maintains complete outreach records in memory.

---

### ~~Notion / Google Docs~~

**With Connector:** Seamless document collaboration for resumes, cover letters, and research notes.

**Without Connector:** You manage documents independently. The plugin generates tailored content for each role and stores versions in memory.

---

## Getting Started

Start using the Job Search Co-Pilot today with `/job-search:start`. As you scale your search, connect tools that match your workflow. Each integration removes manual steps and deepens the plugin's understanding of your opportunities.

Want to suggest a connector? The plugin learns from your feedback and can guide you toward the tools that work best for your job search.
